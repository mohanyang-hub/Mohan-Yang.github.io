Mohan Yang

I am Mohan Yang, a first-year graduate student at the College of Land Science and Technology, China Agricultural University. My research focuses on agricultural remote sensing, with a current focus on integrating 3D crop modeling and hyperspectral data analysis to advance the monitoring and management of sugar beet diseases. On this site, you'll find updates on my research projects, methodological notes and progress toward precision agriculture applications.
